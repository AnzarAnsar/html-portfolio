My top 3 movies of all time

Bilal: A New Breed of Hero (2015)

This movie talks about racial discrimation that was much prevalent 1400 years ago and how the human trafficking was at peak at that time. The main character i.e. Bilal comes out of all this and at the climax, he rise victorious over all those social evils.

Interstellar (2014)

In Earth's future, a global crop blight and second Dust Bowl are slowly rendering the planet uninhabitable. Professor Brand (Michael Caine), a brilliant NASA physicist, is working on plans to save mankind by transporting Earth's population to a new home via a wormhole. But first, Brand must send former NASA pilot Cooper (Matthew McConaughey) and a team of researchers through the wormhole and across the galaxy to find out which of three planets could be mankind's new home.

Lion Movie: The Real Story of Saroo Brierley and His Journey

Lion is indeed based on a true story. It is the adaptation of a non-fiction book named A Long Way Home written by Saroo Brierley. The book is an account of Saroo's accidental separation from his family in India and his adoption by an Australian couple, and his eventual search for his biological family.
